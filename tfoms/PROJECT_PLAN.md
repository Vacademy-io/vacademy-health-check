# Project Plan: T-FOMS (Telangana Forest Offense Management System)

Based on the tender requirements and research, this document outlines the comprehensive plan to design, develop, and deploy the T-FOMS system. The core objective is to digitize the end-to-end forest offense lifecycle, from the Preliminary Offense Report (POR) to the filing of the Chargesheet.

## 1. Project Objectives

- **Digitize "First Response"**: Empower field staff (FBOs/FSOs/Taskforce) with a "Digital Lathi" to report offenses instantly, even offline.
- **Streamline Legal Workflow**: Automate the decision matrix for **Compounding vs. Prosecution** based on the _AP Forest Offences Rules, 1969_.
- **Hierarchical Integrity**: Reflect the distinct reporting lines for Territorial Divisions (Khammam/Sathupally) and the **Taskforce**.
- **Data-Driven Decision Making**: Use GIS and Analytics to identify hotspots and prevent offenses.
- **Integrity & Security**: Ensure evidence authenticity through restricted camera usage and biometric security.

## 2. Core Functional Modules

### A. Mobile Application ("The Digital Lathi")

_Target Users: Forest Beat Officers (FBO), Forest Section Officers (FSO), Taskforce Teams_

**Key Features:**

1.  **Digitized POR Creation**:
    - **POR Numbering**: Auto-generated by Calendar Year (on par with Police FIR) for easier identification.
    - **Offense Categories**: Specific selection for Encroachment, Wildlife, Forest Produce with vehicle, etc.
    - **Data Entry**: Includes "Pancha witness description" and "Auto-generate FBO Report".
    - **Voice-to-Text AI**: Multilingual NLP (Telugu/English) to dictate incident details.
    - **Offline "Store-and-Forward"**: Critical for deep forest beats (e.g., Tallada Range).
    - **Forms Automation**:
      - **Form A**: Preliminary Offense Report (POR).
      - **Form B**: Report of Seizure.
2.  **Evidence & Custody**:
    - **Form C Integration**: Digital generation of "Form C" (Certificate of Safe Custody) for FBOs.
    - **Investigation Support**: Tools to log Eyewitnesses, Expert reports (CCMB, PME), and Call analysis.
    - **Geo-Fenced Evidence**: Auto-capture of GPS coordinates and timestamp.
    - **Secure Media**: Camera-only capture (no gallery upload).
3.  **Security**:
    - **Biometric Login**: Fingerprint/Face ID authentication.

### B. Admin & Command Portal ("The HQ")

_Target Users: DFO, FDOs (Divisional), FROs (Rangers), Dy.ROs, PCCF_

**Key Features:**

1.  **Legal Case Management (Rules 1969)**:
    - **Compounding Decision Engine**:
      - Authority Checks: **FRO** limited to < Rs. 500 value (Rule 8-ii).
      - **Red Flags**: Auto-escalation for "Sandalwood", "Red Sanders", and "Fast-moving vehicles" (FRO cannot compound these - Rule 8-iii).
    - **Form Generation**:
      - **Form A**: Preliminary Offense Report (POR).
      - **Form B**: Seizure Report.
      - **Form C**: Safe Custody Certificate.
      - **Form D**: Undertaking by accused (Rule 5).
      - **Form E**: Order of Compounding.
      - **Form F**: Charge Sheet (Compounding Cases).
    - **Workflow Routes**: Distinct paths for _Compounding_ (Payment & Release) vs. _Prosecution_ (Court Filing).
2.  **Dashboard & Reviews**:
    - **Universal Visibility**: Instant visibility of registered PORs (irrespective of status) to FSO/DyRO, FRO, FDO, and DFO logins.
    - **Hierarchy View**: Drill-down views by **Division** (Khammam/Sathupally) -> **Range** -> **Section** -> **Beat**.
    - **Taskforce Module**: Special workflow for Taskforce FRO -> Dy.RO -> Team investigations.
3.  **GIS & Analytics**:
    - **Heatmaps**: Visualization of offense hotspots (e.g., Teak smuggling in Sathupally).
    - **Trend Analysis**: Frequency of specific offense types.

## 3. Detailed Use Cases

### 1. Digitize the Offense Reporting (POR)

- **Scenario**: FBO detects illegal timber in Sathupally Range.
- **Workflow**:
  1.  **Capture**: FBO captures photos of timber and accused.
  2.  **Seizure**: App generates digital **Form A** (POR), **Form B** (Report of Seizure), and **Form C** (Safe Custody to Watcher/Guard).
  3.  **Submit**: Data syncs to the Range Office.

### 2. Case Disposal: Compounding vs. Prosecution

- **Scenario**: Accused admits guilt; FSO recommends compounding.
- **Workflow**:
  1.  **Assessment**: System calculates value of seized goods.
  2.  **Authority Check**:
      - If Value < Rs 500 AND NOT (Sandalwood/Red Sanders/Vehicles): **FRO** can approve **Form E** (Compounding Order).
      - If Value > Rs 500 OR Sandalwood/Red Sanders: Auto-escalate to **FDO/DFO**.
  3.  **Undertaking**: Accused signs digital/printed **Form D** (Undertaking).
  4.  **Disposal Actions**:
      - **Compoundable**: Collect **C Fees**, auto-generate **C.F 140**, and upload Authorized Officer proceedings.
      - **Non-Compoundable**: Upload Court Orders (Acquittal or Conviction).
  5.  **Release**: Seized property released (unless confiscated).
  6.  _Alternative Path_: If accused refuses or offense is grave -> Generate **Charge Sheet** for Court.

### 3. Approvals & Hierarchical Workflow

- **Level 1: FBO Role**:
  - Issue **Form A (POR)**, **Form B (Seizure)**, and **Form C (Safe Custody)**.
  - Record Pancha witness description.

- **Level 2: DyRO / FSO Role**:
  - **Receipt**: Receive POR, Form A/B/C from FBO.
  - **Investigation Action**:
    - Collect evidences: Eyewitness, Expert Opinions (CCMB, PME for wildlife), Call Analysis.
    - Prepare **63B Certificate** for technical evidence.
    - Issue **35 BNSS (41A CrPC)** notice to accused.
    - Collect **CrPC 161 Statements** of LWs/Prosecution Witnesses.
  - **Charge Sheet**: Prepare Charge Sheet for non-wildlife offenses (self) or assist FRO.
  - **Forwarding**: Send non-compoundable reports to FRO for prosecution orders.

- **Level 3: FRO Role**:
  - **Receipt**: Receive Offence reports (POR, Panchanama, Forms).
  - **Compounding**: Auto-generate **Form F** (Charge sheet in compounding), forward to FDO/Authorized Officer.
  - **Prosecution**: Send report to FDO for Prosecution Orders if non-compoundable.
  - **Confession**: Record accused confession.
  - **Charge Sheet**: Prepare Charge Sheet for wildlife offenses.

- **Level 4: FDO / DFO / Authorized Officer**:
  - Issue Prosecution Orders.
  - Final Authority on compounding major offenses.
  - Universal oversight of all PORs.
- **Taskforce Branch**: Parallel workflow for Taskforce FRO -> Dy.RO -> Team raids.

### 4. Communication & Notifications

- **Alerts**: SMS/Push to DFO if "Sandalwood" or "Red Sanders" is selected (Critical Offense).
- **Reminders**: Nudges for pending Form E issuance > 48 hours.
- **Appeals**: Notification to Sub-DFO/DFO when an appeal is filed against a compounding order.

### 5. Admin Portal Management

- **User Management**: Map users to the specific hierarchy (Khammam Division vs Sathupally Division).
- **Master Data**: Manage compounding rates, protected species lists.
- **Audit Logs**: Track every view and edit for legal non-repudiation.

## 4. Technical Architecture (Tender Compliant)

| Component          | Technology               | Rationale                                             |
| :----------------- | :----------------------- | :---------------------------------------------------- |
| **Backend**        | Java Spring Boot         | Enterprise security, scalability, tender requirement. |
| **Database**       | PostgreSQL + PostGIS     | Robust relational data + Geospatial capabilities.     |
| **AI Service**     | Python Microservice      | Best ecosystem for NLP and Voice-to-Text processing.  |
| **Web Frontend**   | ReactJS                  | Dynamic, responsive dashboards ("The HQ").            |
| **Mobile App**     | Flutter / Native Android | Cross-platform potential or deep native integration.  |
| **Infrastructure** | Docker / Kubernetes      | Containerized deployment for scalability.             |

## 5. Implementation Roadmap

- **Phase 1: Foundation (Weeks 1-2)**
  - Setup Infrastructure (DB, API Gateway).
  - Develop Master Data Management.
  - Build "Digital Lathi" prototype (Offline sync & Basic Form).

- **Phase 2: Core Workflow (Weeks 3-4)**
  - Implement Voice-to-Text integration.
  - Build Admin Dashboard (Kanban view).
  - Enable POR → Chargesheet data flow.

- **Phase 3: Intelligence & Output (Weeks 5-6)**
  - Develop GIS Heatmaps.
  - Implement PDF Generation Engine.
  - UAT (User Acceptance Testing) with sample data.

- **Phase 4: Deployment & Go-Live**
  - Pilot deployment in Sathupally Division.
  - Training of Forest Guards.
  - Full system Go-Live.
